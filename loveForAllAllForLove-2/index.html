not on fighting the old,

everyone.

Awareness of one's ignorance is...

Everybody has two eyes...

Material
things are merely decors of life...

has a heart like them!!!

Before attempting to change or influence the world...
One must first begin by changing
or improving oneself!!!

One cannot control the behavior of others...
But one can always choose how to respond to it!!!

To defeat the darkness out there...
One must defeat the darkness in oneself!!!

Above all...
One must not lie to oneself!!!

Don't let the ugly in others...
Destroy the beauty in Thee!!!

Make the days count!!!

of all wisdom.

Kill the Ego!!!

Be a good person...
But don't waste time trying to prove it.

The greatest enemy of knowledge
is not ignorance...
It is the illusion of knowledge.

One must become addicted,
to bettering oneself!!!

A sign of intelligence is...
An awareness of one's own ignorance!!!

If one could erase all the mistakes of one's past,
one would also erase the wisdom of one's present...
Remember the lesson,
not the disappointment!!!

One must correct one's mistake as soon
as one has found it!!!

A society grows great,
when old men plant trees whose shade they know
they shall never sit in.

I am still learning...

Good people...
If you can't find one...
Be one!!!
And if you can find one...
Cherish it!!!

Every problem has a solution...

Maybe it's a trueth to be accepted!!!

it's not as easy to just give up!!!

Good timber does not grow with ease...

the stronger the trees!!!

But noble souls,
through dust and heat,
Rise from desaster and defeat the stronger!!!

We make a living by what we get...
But we make a life by what we give.

People grow through experience,
if they meet life honestly and courageously...
This is how the character is built.

Use what talent you posses...
The woods would be very silent if no birds sang there,

Life is really simple...

A life lived for others is a life worthwhile.

Life is not about finding yourself...

Which way we choose to see our life in the future...
Depends on our attitude today!!!

Our prime purpose in this life is to help others...
And if we can't help them, at least don't hurt them.

Life's most persistent and urgent question is...
What are we doing for others?

Hate is the venom that consumes its host.

A greatefull heart is better than a bitter soul.

Don't be afraid of death...
Be afraid of an unlived life.
We dont have to live forever...
We just have to live.

In the end,
it's not the years in our life that count.
It's the life in our years!!!

Life is not only a process of discovery...
But also a process of creation.

It's not so much what we have in this life that matters...
It's what we do with what we have.

Its not how long...
But how well we live that matters.

We are here to add what we can to life...
Not to get what we can from it!!!

Be such a pearson, and live such a life...

and every life a life like yours...
The earth would be God's paradise.

The quality of life is more important than life itself.

Life is not measured by length...
But by depth...
Birthdays tell us how long we have been on the road...
Not how far we have travelled.

Sow an action and you reap a habit...

Sow a character and you reap a destiny!!!
