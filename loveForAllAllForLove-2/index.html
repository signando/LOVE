Love for all/ All for Love

Truth is not what one wants it to be...
It is what it is...
And one must bend to its power,
Or live a lie!!!

The only difference between a flower and a weed
is Judgment.

Ponder:
We don't really hear what people say...
We imagine what they mean.

The secret to change is...
To focus all of one's energy,
Not on fighting the old,
But on building the new!!!

Life gets a lot more beautiful.
Once one starts living for oneself.
And accept the fact that one can not please
Everyone.

Awareness of one's own ignorance is...
The beginning of wisdom!!!

Everybody has two eyes...
But no one has the same view!!!

Material Things are merely decors of life...
Not the essence...
Never the purpose...
And certainly not the basis
Of one's existence!!!

If one cannot do great things...
One should do small things in a great way!!!

Innocent people are not fools...
They just think that everyone
Has a heart like them!!!

Before attempting to change or influence the world...
One must first begin by changing
Or improving oneself!!!

One cannot control the behavior of others...
But one can always choose how to respond to it!!!

To defeat the darkness out there...
One must defeat the darkness in oneself!!!

Above all...
One must not lie to oneself!!!

Don't let the ugly in others...
Destroy the beauty in Thee!!!

It is unrealistic to expect people to see you.
As you see yourself.

The greatest killer of happiness,
Is comparison...
If one must compare...
Compare oneself to the person one was yesterday!!!

The only person one should try to be better then...
Is the person one was yesterday!!!

Don't count the days...
Make the days count!!!

It's not about being better than someone else...
It's about being better than you was the day before!!!

The most important journey one can take
Is one of self-discovery...
Knowing oneself is the beginning.
Of all wisdom.

Instead of fighting the world...
Kill the Ego!!!

Be a good person...
But don't waste time trying to prove it.

The greatest enemy of knowledge
Is not ignorance...
It is the illusion of knowledge.

One must become addicted,
To better oneself!!!

A sign of intelligence is...
An awareness of one's own ignorance!!!

If one could erase all the mistakes of one's past,
One would also erase the wisdom of one's present...
Remember the lesson,
Not the disappointment!!!

One must correct one's mistake as soon.
As one has found it!!!

A society grows great,
When old men plant trees whose shade they know
They shall never sit in.

I am still learning...

Good people...
If you can't find one...
Be one!!!
And if you can find one...
Cherish it!!!

Every problem has a solution...
It may sometimes just need another perspective...
And if there seems to be no solution...
Maybe it's a truth to be accepted!!!

To find a mountain path all by oneself,
Gives a greater feeling of strength and satisfaction.
Than to take the path that is shown.

It's easy to give up at the beginning of something,
But after gaining a bit of momentum...
It's not as easy to just give up!!!

Good timber does not grow with ease...
The stronger the wind,
The stronger the trees!!!

But noble souls,
Through dust and heat,
Rise from disaster and defeat the stronger!!!

We make a living by what we get...
But we make a life by what we give.

People grow through experience,
If they meet life honestly and courageously...
This is how the character is built.

Use what talent you possess...
The woods would be very silent if no birds sang there,
except those that sang best!!!

Life is really simple...
But we insist on making it complicated.

A life lived for others is a life worthwhile.

Life is not about finding yourself...
Life is about creating yourself.

Which way do we choose to see our life in the future...
Depends on our attitude today!!!

Our prime purpose in this life is to help others...
And if we can't help them, at least don't hurt them.

Life's most persistent and urgent question is...
What are we doing for others?

Hate is the venom that consumes its host.

A grateful heart is better than a bitter soul.

Don't be afraid of death...
Be afraid of an unlived life.
We don't have to live forever...
We just have to live.

In the end,
It's not the years in our lives that count.
It's the life in our years!!!

Life is not only a process of discovery...
But also a process of creation.

It's not so much what we have in this life that matters...
It's what we do with what we have.

It's not how long...
But how well we live that matters.

We are here to add what we can to life...
Not to get what we can from it!!!

Be such a person, and live such a life...
That if every person was such as you,
And every life a life like yours...
The earth would be God's paradise.

The quality of life is more important than life itself.

Life is not measured by length...
But by depth...
Birthdays tell us how long we have been on the road...
Not how far we have traveled.

Sow an action and you reap a habit...
Sow a habit and you reap a character...
Sow a character and you reap a destiny!!!
